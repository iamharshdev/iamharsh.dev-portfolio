Project Goal

The goal of the project is to create a beautiful and extensible experience for front-end web developers, neutron is based on ace.js, html, css, js and it provides codepen like experience on your local machines.

In the future, we anticipate the community will come up with innovative additions to enhance what could be the simplest, most powerful and well-tested interface for productivity.
